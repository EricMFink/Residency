\subsubsection{Overview}\label{overview}

The Residency program includes both a field experience and a class
component. The class component is an integral part of the work for which
you earn academic credit. As your faculty supervisor, I will review your
weekly assignments and meet with you periodically as a group and
individually to discuss your field experience. I will also communicate
with your site supervisor about your progress.

During your residency, you are bound by the professional conduct rules
and other standards governing lawyers in your jurisdiction and practice
setting. You are expected to resolve any workplace problems with the
attorney or others involved. If you cannot resolve the issue yourself,
you should discuss it with me. If necessary, we will involve the
Residency Program Director.

\subsubsection{Learning Goals}\label{learning-goals}

The Residency course is intended to advance these learning goals:

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  Integrate substantive learning with practical experience, assimilate
  knowledge from the study of law to the practice of law, apply legal
  doctrines learned in class to a practice setting, and bring knowledge
  and skills developed in practice back to the classroom;
\item
  Deepen knowledge of the legal profession and lawyers' professional
  responsibilities and enhance professional development;
\item
  Increase ability to learn from experience, through feedback from
  attorneys, self-evaluation, and reflection on practice experience
  under faculty guidance.
\end{itemize}

Each student will also identify individual goals and develop a plan for
attaining these goals in the residency.

generally not be granted for other reasons absent compelling

Unless specifically authorized by the faculty or site supervisor, you
may not use generative AI services in any work required for this course.

\subsubsection{Grading}\label{grading}

The course will be graded on a pass/fail basis, based on these
standards:

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  Completion of required field placement hours;
\item
  Attendance \& participation in group meetings and individual
  conferences;
\item
  Timely submission of all forms, reports, and time logs; and
\item
  Professionalism in dealing with your field supervisor, faculty
  supervisor, clients, and others during the residency.
\end{itemize}

Exemplary performance in your field placement, assignments, and group
meetings may earn a High Pass. Failure to meet these standards may
result in a Low Pass or a failing grade.
